This Cookie Policy explains how HaloAesthetic uses cookies and similar technologies on haloaesthetic.com (the "Website").

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the website function, remember your preferences, and understand how visitors use the site.

2. Cookies We Use

TypePurpose
EssentialRequired for core functionality, such as account login, cart, and booking sessions.
PreferenceRemembers your cookie consent choice and display preferences.
AnalyticsHelps us understand how visitors use the Website so we can improve it.

3. Your Choices

When you first visit the Website, you are asked to accept or reject non-essential cookies via our cookie banner. You may change your preference at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for this site or adjusting your browser's cookie settings.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be placed by third-party services we use to operate the Website, such as analytics or payment providers. These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies.

5. Changes to This Policy
