Studio Information

Welcome to Halo Aesthetics

Halo Aesthetics is a private, by-appointment beauty studio located at 800 N Pearl St, Unit 401, Denver, CO 80203 (Building Access Code: 8606). We proudly offer professional aesthetic services both in the comfort of your home and at our private Denver studio.

Our mission is to provide a personalized beauty experience through expert facials, threading, waxing, and henna services in a calm, welcoming environment where every appointment is focused entirely on you.

The Halo Philosophy

Let Us Create Your Personalized Experience

Halo Aesthetics is a women-owned boutique aesthetics studio dedicated to delivering exceptional, personalized care in an atmosphere of comfort, privacy, and refinement. Whether you choose to visit our private Denver studio or enjoy our mobile services in the comfort of your home, every appointment is thoughtfully tailored to your individual needs.

Guided by professionalism, attention to detail, and genuine hospitality, we strive to create an experience that leaves you feeling refreshed, confident, and cared for long after your visit.

Minor Policy

Guests under 18 must have a parent or legal guardian complete a consent form. Ages 12–15 require a guardian present during the appointment; ages 16–17 require a signed consent form only. Certain treatments may not be available for clients under 18.

Appointment Policy

Please arrive 5–10 minutes before your scheduled appointment. Late arrivals may result in a shortened service to avoid delaying other guests.
