About Halo Aesthetic

Salon-quality waxing, threading, and facials, in a private studio of your own.

Why I Started Halo

Beauty Care, Without the Noise

Halo Aesthetic was founded by Sofia Zamani with a simple belief: getting waxed, threaded, or treated shouldn't mean a crowded salon, a rushed technician, or a long wait. Halo is a private, by-appointment studio in Denver, where every visit is one-on-one.

Sofia is certified in waxing and threading technique, so you get salon-level results in a calm, personal space — never feeling like just another appointment on the books.
