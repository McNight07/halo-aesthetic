About Halo Aesthetic

An intimate studio devoted to thoughtful, salon-quality waxing, threading, and facial care — entirely your own.

Why Halo Began

Beauty Care, Without the Noise

Halo Aesthetic was founded by Sofia Zamani on a simple conviction: care like this deserves quiet, not a crowded room or a clock-watching technician. Halo is an intimate, by-appointment studio in Denver, where every visit unfolds one-on-one, at an unhurried pace.

Sofia is certified in advanced waxing and threading technique, bringing salon-level artistry into a calm, personal space — so you never feel like simply another name on the books.
